Does vitamin C prevent colds?
V
Partially Verified

Regular vitamin C supplementation does not prevent colds in the general population, but it may reduce cold duration and severity by about 8-14%. For people under high physical stress (like marathon runners), vitamin C may reduce cold incidence by up to 50%.

Sources: Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews, Journal of Nutrition Science (2022)
